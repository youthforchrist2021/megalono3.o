Who We Are

A Spirit-filled community set apart to carry the anointing of Christ to the next generation — called, equipped and sent.

Founded in 2021

Our Story

Born from a call,
built on faith.

Anointed Youth for Christ (AYC) was born out of a simple but powerful conviction — that God has a specific call and anointing upon the youth of this generation. Founded in 2021, AYC began as a small gathering of passionate, Spirit-filled young believers in Kerala who believed God was calling them to something greater than themselves.

What started as a prayer circle quickly grew into a full-fledged youth ministry — hosting events, Bible studies, camps and worship nights that have impacted hundreds of lives. We are not just a group; we are a movement of young people set apart for God's purposes.

At AYC, we believe every young person is uniquely appointed by God — to lead, to worship, to serve and to transform the world around them. We are a community that challenges each other, holds each other accountable and walks together in the journey of faith.

What We Stand On

Our Three Pillars

01

Prayer & Intercession

We are a community of intercessors. Prayer is not just an activity — it is our foundation. From weekly prayer cells to all-night prayer sessions, we seek God first in all things.

02

Biblical Grounding

Through WAY LIGHT Bible Study, Logos and personal devotion, we anchor ourselves in God's Word. We believe the Bible is alive and active, and able to transform every area of life.

03

Talent & Gift Development

We believe every youth carries a God-given talent — music, leadership, media, teaching and more. AYC creates platforms for these gifts to be developed and deployed for the Kingdom.

What We Do

Areas of Ministry

Asaphians — Music Ministry

Named after the chief musician of Israel, Asaphians is our worship ministry. We believe music is a prophetic weapon and a gateway to God's presence. Our team leads worship at events, services and community gatherings, training young musicians and worshippers to serve with excellence.

Youth Ministry

The heartbeat of AYC — we exist to empower young people to grow in Christ. Through regular programs, camp, mentorship and community, we create an environment where youth are spiritually nourished, emotionally supported and relationally connected.

Prayer Cell

Intercessory prayer is central to everything we do. Our prayer cells meet regularly — bringing needs before God and standing in the gap for the community, city and nation. We believe prayer changes everything.

Child Ministry

Planting seeds of faith in the youngest generation. Our child ministry creates age-appropriate spaces where children encounter God's love, learn His Word, and grow in joyful, active faith from their earliest years.

Online Activity & Media

We carry the Gospel into digital spaces. Through our media ministry, we produce worship content, share testimonies and run online Bible studies (WAY LIGHT) — reaching youth wherever they are, including via YouTube, Instagram and WhatsApp.

Talent Development

AYC believes every believer is gifted. We create platforms — from events to workshops — that discover, develop and deploy the unique talents of each member for God's glory. No gift is too small for the Kingdom.

Our Journey

Walking in Faith since 2021

2021
AYC is Founded

A group of Spirit-filled youth in Kerala gather with a vision to build a community set apart for God. AYC is officially established with its first leadership team.

2022
WAY LIGHT Bible Study Launches

The WAY LIGHT online Bible study goes live on WhatsApp — every Wednesday and Thursday. Hundreds of youth join the weekly sessions, studying the Word together.

2023
Megalono & Logos — Flagship Events

Megalono, AYC's annual gathering, makes its debut — drawing youth from across the region for worship, the Word and fellowship. Logos Bible Study is launched as a structured deep-dive program.

2024
Asaphians Music Ministry & AYC Camp

The Asaphians music ministry is formally launched, and the first AYC Camp is held — a landmark moment of community, adventure and spiritual breakthrough for our youth.

2025
Megalono 3.0 & Logos 2.0 — The Next Chapter

AYC enters its next season with 120+ members, Megalono 3.0 and Logos 2.0 on the horizon, and a growing digital presence. The best is yet to come.
