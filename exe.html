About the Camp

A day to encounter God's presence

AYC Camp is our annual youth gathering — a time to step away from the noise of life and step into the tangible presence of God. Worship, prayer, the Word, fellowship, and adventure await you.

Whether you're a strong believer or just beginning to explore faith — this camp is for you.

Powerful Worship Sessions

Spirit-led live worship with the Asaphians music ministry

Anointed Teaching

In-depth Biblical sessions with our youth leaders and invited speakers

Prayer & Ministry Time

Personal ministry, intercession, and encounter with God

Community & Fellowship

Make lifelong friendships with other anointed youth

Fun Activities

Games, outdoor activities, talent showcase & more!
